ónoma
onómata

Name. Literally, “name”. Can refer to the personal name (whether true or just a nickname), the full name, or anything in between, depending on context. In its entirety, consists of etymṓnymon ḗ prosonymía; patrōnymikós ḗ andrōnymikón ḗ despotṓnymon; diakritōnýmion ḗ kenón; ethnikón. Cognate of ‹name›, ‹‑onym‑›; related to the cognates of ‹nomenclature›, ‹anonymous›. See also: appellation, hypokorismós, epítheton, epṓnymos, Name, pseudṓnymon.
